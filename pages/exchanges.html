Exchange and Return Policy

Fashion Street uses state-of-the-art technology to manufacture its products, striving for quality and customer satisfaction. For respect and to maintain the credibility gained with its consumers, the company has created a return and exchange policy in accordance with the Code of Consumer Protection, and concerned that you (the customer) get an effective negotiation, fast and especially satisfactory.

If you choose to contact us by e-mail or telephone, the form will be sent to you to be filled out and sent together with the item(s). The product returned without this form and/or without informing the Customer Service Department will be returned without prior consultation.

When making the return/exchange process, the customer must, on the back of the invoice to be returned/exchanged, inform the reason for the return/exchange, the name of the person who is returning, social security number, and the date of return.

If you receive a product with violated packaging, refuse it upon delivery.

If the quantity received differs from the invoice, please contact us through one of the channels available at the bottom of this site.

*ATTENTION: To make the exchange process, you must be logged in.

Return for Regret/Withdrawal

If upon receiving the product, you decide to return it for regret, you must do so within seven calendar days from the date of receipt, observing the following conditions:

  • The product may not have any signs of use.
  • The product should preferably be sent in its original packaging, accompanied by the invoice, labels, tags (label with product reference code) properly attached to the product and all its accessories.
  • When making the return process, the customer must, on the back of the invoice to be returned, inform the reason for refusal/return, the name and social security number of the person who is returning and the date of return.
