Who we are

Fashion Street is a virtual store based on the Quitanda Online store (e-commerce), it is a fictitious store created like study for the course "Desenvolvimento de uma loja virtual com Bootstrap 5 e ASP.NET 5" available for free by Mr Ricardo Maroquio on his Youtube channel.

The source code can be obtained via Github, According to the guidelines presented thoughout the course in the Youtube videos, this study can be used freely as a basis for other virtual stores, at no cost to the user.
